Setting the remote port

The remote port is the port on which WS_FTP will try to connect to the FTP site. This is normally 21; however, it may be something different if you are going through a firewall, or if the FTP site administrator has assigned the FTP protocol to another port.

  1. In the toolbar, click Connect to open the Site Manager.

  2. Select the site in the Sites list, then click Edit.

  3. Select the Advanced dialog.

  4. Enter the Remote Port number.
