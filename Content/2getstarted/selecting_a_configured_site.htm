Connecting to a configured site

FTP sites you have configured appear in the Sites list on the Site Manager. You can connect to a site in the following ways:

  1. In the toolbar, click Connect to open the Site Manager.

  2. Select a site in the Sites list, then click Connect.

OR

  1. In the Site Manager, double-click on a site in the Sites list.

OR

  1. In the toolbar, click on the down arrow next to Connect, then select a site.

OR    

  1. From the File menu, select Connect, then select the site from the sites list.
