Changing the current drive

You can change the drive on your PC (the local system).

Do one of the following:

  • Type the drive letter followed by a colon into the current folder box; for example, d:

  • Scroll the list box and click the appropriate drive.

  • Click the Change Folder button. When you are prompted for a folder to change to, enter the drive letter followed by a colon (for example, d:), and then click the OK button.

  • Click the Browse (...) button next to the folder and select the drive from the list.
