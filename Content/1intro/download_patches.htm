Downloading Software Patches and Upgrades

Ipswitch occasionally makes patches and upgrades available on our Web site. For the latest version information, please visit www.ipswitch.com. Note that product upgrades to extend capabilities are also available from the same locations, but you should note that most upgrades require a valid service agreement.

 
  1. To access patches on the Ipswitch Web site:
  2. In your Web browser, go to: http://www.ipswitch.com
  3. Click the Service & Support link.
  4. Click WS_FTP.
  5. Click Patches and Upgrades, and then click the link for your platform type.
  6. Save the patch file to a location other than your WS_FTP folder or your Temp folder.
  7. Run the patch file to update the software.
