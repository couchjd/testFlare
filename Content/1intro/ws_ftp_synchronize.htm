Synchronize Utility

Synchronize is a utility that can "mirror" files and folders between your PC and an FTP site. This is especially useful for quickly and efficiently updating your Web site by synchronizing your web space on your ISP's FTP site with your web folder on your PC.

To use the Synchronize Utility, select Synchronize Utility from the Tools menu.
